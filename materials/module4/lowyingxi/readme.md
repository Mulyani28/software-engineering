# Software Requirements Document (SRD): Event Calendar for UTM

Define the purpose and scope: Decide on the purpose of the calendar and who the target audience is. Will it be for current students, faculty, staff, alumni, or the general public? Will it cover all events on campus or focus on specific types of events?

Choose a platform: Decide on the platform you will use to host and manage the calendar. This could be a pre-existing calendar tool or a custom solution.

Gather event information: Collect all necessary information about each event, including the name, date, time, location, description, and any other pertinent details.

Design the calendar: Design the calendar with the end-user in mind. Consider the layout and user experience to make it easy to navigate and find events.

Populate the calendar: Add all the events to the calendar, making sure to include all relevant information.

Promote the calendar: Promote the calendar to the target audience through various channels such as social media, email newsletters, flyers, and posters.

Maintain and update the calendar: Regularly update and maintain the calendar to keep it current and accurate. Assign someone to manage the calendar and review event submissions regularly to ensure it stays up to date.
